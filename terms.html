1. Booking & Deposit

To confirm your boutique travel experience with Tusker Ways, a non-refundable deposit of 30% of the total tour cost is required. Your booking is only considered "confirmed" once this payment is received and we issue a formal booking confirmation.

2. Balance Payment

The remaining 75% balance is to be settled upon your arrival in Sri Lanka.

Payment Methods: We accept payments via Cash (USD/EUR/LKR) or Credit/Debit Cards.

Note: For card payments, a standard bank surcharge may apply.
