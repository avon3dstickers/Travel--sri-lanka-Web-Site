The Forest Walk

Walk alongside these magnificent creatures at their own pace through lush riverside trails. A gentle, ethical alternative that respects the elephant's natural dignity.

Traditional Care

River Care

Participate in the centuries-old traditional bathing ritual in the Maha Oya River. Help cleanse and cool the elephants in a fully voluntary, joyful experience.

Sustainable

Eco-Innovation

Visit the Maximus Dung Paper Factory, transforming elephant dung into odour-free stationery. Supporting a circular economy that provides vital sanctuary income.
